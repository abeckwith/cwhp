The Cambridge Women's Heritage Project

  1. CWHP's goal is to write women into the history of Cambridge, Mass., and to honor their lives and recognize their many accomplishments
  2. We celebrate the contributions of women from the early days of Cambridge to the present
  3. We currently have over 900 women and women's organizations that have been nominated for a place in the database
  4. We welcome your nominations, your corrections, or your time to help us research, edit, and improve our database!
